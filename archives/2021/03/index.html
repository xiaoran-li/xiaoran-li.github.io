My Detail CV.

From 2020 to 2021, I was engaged in the study of word vector representation in the Natural Language Processing Laboratory of Shizuoka Institute of Science and Technology (SIST). From April 2021, I transferred to the Adaptive Systems Laboratory (Machine Learning Laboratory) to continue my research on natural language processing.


Research Interest

Word embedding representation; Sememe representation; Multi-language fusion representation learning; Unsupervised machine translation; Natural language processing; machine learning.
